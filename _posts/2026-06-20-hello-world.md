I spend most of my days with simulations and statistics, and I wanted one small
place that wasn't about any of that — somewhere to put the things I make and the
things I'm learning for the fun of it.

So this is that place. Expect occasional posts, a slowly growing wall of
paintings, and probably some half-finished thoughts about learning Finnish.

To write a new post, I just drop a Markdown file into the `_posts` folder named
like `YYYY-MM-DD-some-title.md`, add a short header at the top, and write. That's
the whole workflow — which is the point. If it's easy, I'll actually keep it up.
